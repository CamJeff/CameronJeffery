About me

I’m a physics undergraduate at McGill University, originally from Calgary. At first, I wanted to work in medicine, but after taking my first cell biology class at McGill, I quickly realized that subject wasn't for me, and swapped over to physics. Since then, I’ve found that physics offers the kind of problem-solving I enjoy most. I’m also completing a minor in mathematics, which has helped deepen and supplement my understanding of the subject of physics in many ways.

Looking ahead, I hope to work in medical physics, as it's a path that connects my early interest in medicine with the technical tools of physics.
