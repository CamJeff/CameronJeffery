Fiddler on the Proof

Fiddler on the Proof is blog that offers a math puzzle every Friday, and you are given the weekend to solve it. They offer a nice little challenge, and if you are a paid subscriber, you get your name and the number of points you acrue on a leaderboard.

The problems seem to be exlusively related to probability or combinatorics, and typically all that is needed is some fundamental knowledge in an 'intro to probabiliy' class to solve all of their questions (perhaps not their extra credit, however). Generally, problems are related to discrete probability, expectation, stochastic processes, and even hints of game theory.

I've been solving the newly released problems quite consistently ever since July 25, 2025 (although sometimes I skip if I don't connect with the problem). I also don't bother with the extra credits usually, since I can't see the solutions they provide the following week (I'm not a paid subscriber).
