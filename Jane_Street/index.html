Jane Street Puzzles

Jane Street is a quantitative trading firm founded in the USA and was introduced to them through their Monthly Puzzles. In fact, for a while, I thought that's all Jane Street was - a website that posts a fun and challenging puzzle each month where solvers can competitively grind out and get their name placed on a leaderboard.

But in fact, Jane Street is so much more; working on tough financial market data problems while instilling an environment and work culture where these sort of puzzles seem integral to the type of problem solving they do on the job.

Intrigued by this, I've started regularly solving each newly released puzzle they put out since my first ever solve on September 2024's Puzzle. They have been an extremely fun challenge for me, and I'm excited to see each new creative release at the beginning of each month.

I try my best to solve every puzzle by hand, though some the puzzles seem to demand a coded solution. These types of puzzles seem to be more tradional "puzzle" type puzzles, where you have to toy and fiddle with pieces that are meant to fit together is a certain way. These kinds of puzzles seem to have so many permutations and variables to consider that it seems impossibly daunting to try it out by hand without the help of some optimal program to solve it for you.

The puzzle solutions I've added here are for puzzles that either demanded a coded solution, or are the puzzles that are much more mathematically involved (i.e they are just a straight up math question). All other puzzles I have solved by hand.
